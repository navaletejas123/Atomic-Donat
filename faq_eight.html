In what ways does the FSVI Program contribute to enhancing the maturity of Active Directory security? 

We understand that a mature AD posture is crucial for safeguarding your digital assets, and our program is designed to provide invaluable insights and proactive measures. Here's how FSVI can enhance your Active Directory security: 

01. Inspection of Misconfiguration.

Identifying and rectifying misconfigurations is the first line of defense against potential security threats. FSVI meticulously inspects your AD environment to pinpoint and address any misconfigurations, ensuring that your system operates at its most secure and efficient level. 

03. Top 20 Attacks Used by Ransomware Groups Against AD.

Stay one step ahead of cyber threats by understanding the tactics employed by ransomware groups. FSVI identifies and analyzes the top 20 attacks used against AD, providing you with the knowledge to fortify your defenses and protect your organization from evolving cyber threats. 

05. Single Sign-On (SSO) & Policy Control Audit of AD

 Ensure the integrity and security of your AD through a comprehensive audit of Single Sign-On (SSO) and policy controls. FSVI Program assesses your AD policies, providing you with a clear understanding of your security landscape and recommendations for enhancing policy controls. 

02. Improper Access Control, Privileges, and User Rights Management.

Maintaining a robust AD posture requires careful management of access controls, privileges, and user rights. FSVI Program delves deep into these critical aspects, offering you insights and recommendations to tighten your security controls, preventing unauthorized access and potential breaches. 

04. Awareness and Training for System Administrators.

Knowledge is power, and FSVI ensures that your system administrators are equipped with the latest information and best practices. Our program includes tailored awareness sessions and training for system administrators, empowering them to take proactive measures and implement effective AD precautions.
